Fleet Google Calendar

warelock

We recently added a public Atlantic R&D Fleet Google Calendar that mirrors all Discord events we schedule. Follow this procedure to add our calendar and never miss another developer voice call/meeting again:

  1. In your web browser: Add the Pirate Chain Atlantic R&D Fleet Discord Scheduled Events Google Calendar here.
  2. In Google Calendar: Look on the left side of the Google Calendar window for the Atlantic Fleet calendar you just added
  3. Hover your mouse over the calendar entry, see the calendar's triple-dot menu, and click it
  4. Settings > Event Notifications > Add notification > Set the default reminder for all Atlantic Fleet events to whatever you want (I suggest 15 minutes)
